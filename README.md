4+ years of production experience building software and digital solutions for automotive and industrial environments — from embedded C/C++ firmware on industrial measurement systems to IoT pipelines running on Volkswagen do Brasil's production lines.

Currently working as a Process Analyst at **Volkswagen do Brasil** (via Engbras), developing IoT traceability solutions, process automations with Microsoft Power Platform, and integrating electronic vehicle systems in high-complexity manufacturing settings.

My background spans:

- **Data Science** — company-sponsored training covering Pandas, NumPy, Scikit-learn, and TensorFlow

Undergraduate thesis awarded **1st place at INOVAFEI 2023**, FEI's annual undergraduate innovation competition.

Currently self-studying Machine Learning and building a public software engineering portfolio. Open to **entry-level Software Engineering** and **ML Engineering** roles — remote or international.
